Startup ideas (pick 1–2 to validate first)
1) “Scam Shield” mobile app (consumer)

What it does: Real-time call/SMS/WhatsApp link risk warnings; detects impersonation scripts (“KYC”, “customs”, “digital arrest”).
Why it can win: Simple habit loop; clear value in seconds.
Monetization: Freemium + family plan; referral fees for identity protection/insurance.

2) UPI “pre-transaction risk check” SDK (B2B2C)

What it does: Plugs into wallets/fintechs to score payee risk (new payee, mismatch signals, velocity anomalies) and adds “friction” only when risky.
Customers: UPI apps, neobanks, merchant PSPs.
Monetization: Per-1,000 checks / platform fee.

3) “Fraud concierge” recovery service (consumer + SMB)

What it does: Guided incident workflow: evidence capture, complaint filing, bank/NPCI escalation templates, timeline tracking.
Why it can win: Victims don’t know what to do in the first hour.
Monetization: Success fee + subscription for SMBs.

4) Business invoice/payment fraud guard for SMEs

What it does: Flags changed bank details, fake vendor emails, and account-takeover attempts; approval workflows on high-risk changes.
Customers: SMEs using Tally/Zoho/ERP-lite.
Monetization: SaaS per seat / per company.

5) “Verified helpline directory” + deepfake/impersonation detector

What it does: One-tap verification of “bank/police/courier” callers; warns on spoofed numbers and scripted intimidation.
GTM: Partnerships with employers, colleges, RWAs (resident welfare associations).

6) Community fraud early-warning network (city/district)

What it does: Aggregates scam patterns by geography/language; pushes alerts (“new courier scam in Pune”).
Monetization: B2G/B2B dashboards for banks, telecoms, and local admins.

7) “SafePay” for senior citizens

What it does: Optional second-approval from a trusted contact for high-value transfers; simplified “panic button” freeze guidance.
Monetization: Family subscription; distribution via hospitals/pharmacies.

8) Fraud risk training that actually changes behavior (for enterprises)

What it does: Short, local-language simulations of current scams; tracks reduction in risky actions.
Monetization: Per-employee per-year; HR/IT security budgets.

Fast way to choose the best one

If you want quick consumer adoption: #1 or #7
If you want higher ARPU and stickiness: #2 or #4
If you want service-led, revenue early: #3
